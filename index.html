Privacy Policy for Konki

Last updated: October 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Konki, a to-do list application developed using React Native and Expo. This Privacy Policy explains how Konki handles user information. Your privacy is important to us — we want you to know that Konki does not collect, share, or store any personal user data.

2. Information We Collect

Konki does not collect any personal information from users. The app does not require your name, email address, location, or any personal identifiers.

All information created within the app (such as tasks and categories) is stored locally on your device using Async Storage. This means your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

3. How We Use Data

Data stored within the app is used solely for functionality, including:

  • Displaying and managing your personal tasks and categories.
  • Preserving task states (completed or pending).

Konki does not analyze, sell, or share any user data.

4. Device Permissions

Konki does not request or require any sensitive device permissions. The app does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or external storage.

5. Third-Party Services

Konki does not use any third-party services or SDKs that collect user data. It does not include advertising, analytics, or external APIs that track user behavior.

6. Data Security

All user data is stored only on your device. We recommend keeping your device protected (e.g., with a passcode or lock screen) since Konki cannot control the physical security of your device.

7. Children’s Privacy

Konki is suitable for all ages. The app does not knowingly collect data from children or any users under any age.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When updates occur, the “Last updated” date at the top will be modified. All changes will be reflected within the app or on the page where this policy is published.
